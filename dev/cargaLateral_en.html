SIDE LOAD DUE TO WIND

Side Load:

Definitions:

Wind Density: Air density (approximately 1.225 kg/m³ at sea level at 15°C).

Reference Area: Area exposed to the wind.

Drag Coefficient: Dimensionless value depending on the shape and orientation of the load (can vary between 0 and 2, depending on the shape of the load).

Wind Speed: Incident wind speed on the load.
